This demonstration UMLS terminology browser and app is loaded with a Level 0 + SNOMEDCT_US edition of the 2014AB UMLS®. It contains complete versions of many terminologies, including SNOMEDCT_US, MeSH, RXNORM, and others. The data set contains over 1.6 million concepts with over 5.5 million individual terms from constituent terminologies.

Due to the use of IP restricted content, we require users to authenticate with an NLM UTS account to ensure they have agreed to the UMLS License. We are a UMLS authorized content distributor.

To obtain a UTS account, visit http://uts.nlm.nih.gov
